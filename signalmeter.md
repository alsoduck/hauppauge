# See Signal Meter(2 tuner) in Hauppauge WinTV-dualHD
1. Download Hauppauge signal monitor.<br><https://www.hauppauge.com/pages/support/support_digital_signal_monitor.html#download>
2. go to `C:\Program Files (x86)\WinTV\Signal Monitor\`
3. Tuner 1: `hcwSigMon.exe -tray`, Tuner 2: `hcwSigMon.exe  -tray -demod2`
